## What is KarmaLinks?

KarmaLinks is a backlink exchange club built specifically for B2B SaaS
companies. Members earn "karma" points by giving backlinks and spend them to
receive backlinks, matched by AI on niche, domain authority, and content
relevance. It uses ABC triangular exchanges (A links to B, B links to C, C
links to A) instead of direct reciprocal links, and requires a minimum
Domain Rating of 15 to join.

## KarmaLinks vs Exchange Backlinks

| | KarmaLinks | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | Backlink exchange only | Backlink matching only |
| Price | Free to start | Free |
| Entry requirement | Minimum DR 15 | None |
| Exchange structure | Points system, ABC triangular | Direct, one-for-one, both sides agree |
| Niche matching | B2B SaaS | Broad B2B niches (marketing, sales, HR, fintech, etc.) |

## Where KarmaLinks still wins

KarmaLinks is closest in spirit to Exchange Backlinks and genuinely free at
the entry tier. If your site clears the DR 15 bar and you specifically want
an automated points marketplace rather than a direct conversation with a
partner site, it's a reasonable option to also try.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

No DR minimum to join — smaller or newer B2B sites are still welcome. And
instead of spending points into a triangular exchange with sites you can't
fully vet upfront, you get matched with one specific site in your niche, see
who they are, and agree on the placement directly before anything goes live.
