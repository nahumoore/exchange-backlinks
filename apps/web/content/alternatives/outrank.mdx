## What is Outrank?

Outrank (Outrank.so) is an AI content automation platform — keyword research,
article generation, and auto-publishing to your CMS. Every subscription
includes a Backlink Exchange: a credit system where you earn credits by
hosting other members' links in your articles, and spend credits to get your
own links placed in theirs. Higher Domain Rating sites earn more credits per
hosted link, and credits reset daily.

## Outrank vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Outrank | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | AI content generation + publishing | Backlink matching only |
| Backlink exchange | Included with a content subscription | The entire product |
| Price | From $99/mo (30 articles) | Free |
| Matching model | Credit marketplace, DR-weighted | Weekly niche-matched email, mutual agreement |
| Niche matching | Cross-industry | B2B niches only |

## Where Outrank still wins

The credit system means links flow continuously without you negotiating each
one — if you're fine with an automated placement network and you're already
paying for the AI content plan anyway, the exchange is a no-cost add-on for
you.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

You shouldn't have to buy an AI writing subscription to get a backlink
exchange. Exchange Backlinks is free on its own, and every match is a real
site in your niche that a human on the other end agreed to — not a credit
auto-spent into an article you didn't write.
