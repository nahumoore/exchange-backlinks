## What is Outrank?

[Outrank](https://www.outrank.so/) is an AI content automation platform — keyword research, article generation, and auto-publishing to your CMS, one article a day up to 30/month, for a flat $99/month. Every subscription includes a Backlink Exchange: a credit system where you earn credits by hosting other members' links in your articles and spend credits to get your own links placed in theirs. Higher Domain Rating sites earn more credits per hosted link, and credits reset daily. Some reviewers have flagged the automated, cross-network placement model as sitting close to what Google's [spam policies](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials/spam-policies) call excessive link exchanges, since links are inserted by an algorithm rather than agreed on case by case.

## Where Outrank still wins

The credit system means links flow continuously without you negotiating each one — if you're fine with an automated placement network and you're already paying for the AI content plan anyway, the exchange is a no-cost add-on. It's a reasonable fit for teams optimizing for content volume first and treating backlinks as a byproduct.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Outrank free?

No. Outrank is $99/month for 30 AI-generated articles; the backlink exchange is bundled into that subscription and isn't sold separately.

### Is Outrank's backlink exchange safe for SEO?

It's automated cross-network link placement, which is the pattern Google's link spam guidance singles out when it happens at scale without human review on each link. That doesn't mean every user gets penalized, but a manually agreed, one-for-one exchange like Exchange Backlinks carries less of that structural risk.

### What's the best free Outrank alternative?

Exchange Backlinks: free, one weekly email matched to your B2B niche, and you and your match agree on the placement together before it goes live. Browse more [free alternatives](/alternatives) if you're comparing several tools.

### Can I use Outrank's backlink exchange without subscribing to its content plan?

No — the exchange is only available as part of a paid Outrank content subscription.
