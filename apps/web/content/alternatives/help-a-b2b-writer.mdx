## What is Help a B2B Writer?

Help a B2B Writer is the mechanic exchange-backlinks is closest to in spirit
— it's free, matches B2B writers with expert sources, and delivers matches
by email digest. The difference is what gets exchanged: writers submit
requests for expert quotes, sources respond, and if used, the source
usually gets a byline mention and a link back — but it's not guaranteed, and
it's not the point of the match.

## Help a B2B Writer vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Help a B2B Writer | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| What's exchanged | Expert quotes for articles | Backlinks, directly |
| Backlink outcome | Possible, at the writer's discretion | Guaranteed by the match — that's the entire deal |
| Match format | Daily/weekly request digests | One weekly email, both sides |
| Price | Free (a paid tier has been discussed but hasn't shipped) | Free |
| Effort per link | Write a genuinely useful quote, hope it's used and linked | Agree with a specific site, place the link |

## Where Help a B2B Writer still wins

If your real goal is media mentions and third-party credibility — not just
a link — being quoted in someone else's article is worth more than a
reciprocal swap. It's also a good complement: a source-request digest and a
backlink exchange aren't mutually exclusive.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

With Help a B2B Writer, getting a backlink depends on a writer deciding to
credit you — it's a byproduct, not a guarantee. Exchange Backlinks is
built around the link itself: you're matched specifically to trade one, and
both sides agree before it goes live.
