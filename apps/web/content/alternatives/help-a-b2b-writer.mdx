## What is Help a B2B Writer?

[Help a B2B Writer](https://helpab2bwriter.com/) is the mechanic Exchange Backlinks is closest to in spirit — it's free, matches B2B writers with expert sources, and delivers matches by email digest. Writers submit requests for expert quotes (up to three per publication per week), tagged by industry, and sources get a digest of relevant ones. The difference is what gets exchanged: if a source's quote is used, they usually get a byline mention and a link back — but it's not guaranteed, and it isn't the point of the match.

## Help a B2B Writer vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Help a B2B Writer | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| What's exchanged | Expert quotes for articles | Backlinks, directly |
| Backlink outcome | Possible, at the writer's discretion | Guaranteed by the match — that's the entire deal |
| Match format | Daily/weekly request digests, tagged by industry | One weekly email, both sides |
| Price | Free (a paid tier has been discussed but hasn't shipped) | Free |
| Effort per link | Write a genuinely useful quote, hope it's used and linked | Agree with a specific site, place the link |

## Where Help a B2B Writer still wins

If your real goal is media mentions and third-party credibility — not just a link — being quoted in someone else's article is worth more than a reciprocal swap. It's also a good complement: a source-request digest and a backlink exchange aren't mutually exclusive, and B2B-focused teams often run both at once.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

With Help a B2B Writer, getting a backlink depends on a writer deciding to credit you — it's a byproduct, not a guarantee. [Submit your site](/submit) to Exchange Backlinks instead, and it's built around the link itself: you're matched specifically to trade one, and both sides agree before it goes live. For other source-request formats, see how [Featured.com](/alternatives/featured) and [HARO](/alternatives/haro) compare.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Help a B2B Writer free?

Yes, for both writers and sources. A paid tier has been discussed publicly but hasn't shipped as of this writing.

### Does responding to a Help a B2B Writer request guarantee a backlink?

No. Whether you get credited — and linked — is entirely up to the writer using your quote. Many responses go unused or get credited without a link.

### What's the best free Help a B2B Writer alternative for guaranteed backlinks?

Exchange Backlinks — the match itself is the deal, so there's no dependency on a writer choosing to credit you. Compare it against the rest of the [free alternatives](/alternatives) if you're weighing several tools.

### Can I use Help a B2B Writer and Exchange Backlinks together?

Yes. They solve different problems — one earns you press mentions and possible links through quotes, the other guarantees a reciprocal backlink through a direct match — and running both isn't redundant.
