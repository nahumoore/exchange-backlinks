## What is Collaborator.pro?

Collaborator.pro is a PR distribution and guest-posting marketplace with a
catalog of 43,000+ websites. It's free to join, but every placement is paid
— prices set by the publisher, ranging from roughly $5 to over $1,000 per
link, with no markup added on top.

## Collaborator.pro vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Collaborator.pro | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core model | Pay-per-placement marketplace | Free reciprocal exchange |
| Price | $5–$1,000+ per link | Free |
| Catalog size | 43,000+ sites, any industry | B2B niche members only |
| Link guarantee | 3-month standard, extendable to 12 | No formal guarantee — arranged between members |
| Speed | ~75% of placements go live within 48 hours | Weekly match cadence |

## Where Collaborator.pro still wins

Collaborator isn't really competing on "free" — it's a marketplace, not an
exchange, and that's its strength. If you have budget and need a link on a
specific site fast, from a catalog of tens of thousands of options across
every industry, Collaborator's scale and speed beat waiting for a niche
match.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

If you don't have a link-buying budget, Collaborator isn't an option —
every placement costs money. Exchange Backlinks is the free path: you give
a link, you get a link, no invoice either way. The trade-off is scale —
you're matched with other B2B members weekly, not browsing 43,000 sites on
demand.
