## What is Collaborator.pro?

[Collaborator.pro](https://collaborator.pro/) is a PR distribution and guest-posting marketplace with a catalog of over 42,000 websites. It's free to join, but every placement is paid — prices are set by the publisher, typically $40–90 per post (up to $1,000+ on premium sites), plus a platform commission on top. About 75% of placements go live within 48 hours, and links come with a 3-month protection guarantee, extendable to 12 months, meaning Collaborator replaces the link at no extra cost if it's removed early.

## Where Collaborator.pro still wins

Collaborator isn't really competing on "free" — it's a marketplace, not an exchange, and that's its strength. If you have budget and need a link on a specific site fast, from a catalog of tens of thousands of options across every industry, Collaborator's scale and speed beat waiting for a niche match. It's the better call when timing matters more than cost.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Collaborator.pro free?

Joining and browsing the catalog is free, but every guest post placement itself is paid — there's no free-tier way to actually get a link.

### How much does a link cost on Collaborator.pro?

Most posts run $40–90, though premium sites can charge $1,000 or more, with the platform adding a commission on top of the publisher's price.

### What's the best free Collaborator.pro alternative?

Exchange Backlinks: no per-link cost, no commission — you trade a link directly with a matched B2B site instead of paying a publisher. See more options in the full list of [free alternatives](/alternatives).

### Does Collaborator.pro guarantee my link stays live?

Yes — a 3-month protection window is standard, extendable to 12 months, and Collaborator replaces the link free of charge if it's taken down within that period.
