## What is Arvow?

Arvow is an AI content and autoblogging platform with an automatic backlink
exchange bundled into its Business plan. Arvow's AI analyzes your content to
find topically relevant sites in the network and places links directly in
article bodies — no manual outreach required.

## Arvow vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Arvow | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | AI content + autoblogging | Backlink matching only |
| Backlink exchange | Bundled into the Business plan | The entire product |
| Price | $249/mo (Business plan) | Free |
| Who places the link | Fully automated | You, after agreeing with your match |
| Niche matching | Cross-industry | B2B niches only |

## Where Arvow still wins

If you're already running Arvow for autoblogging and content generation at
scale, the exchange runs itself in the background with zero extra setup —
useful if link building isn't something you want to think about at all.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

$249/month is a lot to pay for a backlink exchange you can get for free.
Exchange Backlinks also keeps you in the loop — you know exactly which site
you're linking to and why, instead of an AI placing links across your
content automatically.
