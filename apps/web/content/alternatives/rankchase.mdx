## What is RankChase?

RankChase automatically matches you with websites in your niche and Domain
Rating range that are open to backlink exchanges, including 3-way (ABC)
exchanges. The free tier notifies you of matches; a Premium plan
($19/mo/website) unlocks full transparency on who you're matched with, ABC
exchanges, and filtering by DR and traffic.

## RankChase vs Exchange Backlinks

| | RankChase | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | Backlink exchange only | Backlink matching only |
| Price | Free tier + $19/mo/website Premium | Free |
| Seeing who you're matched with | Paid feature | Included from the start |
| Niche coverage | 30+ categories, general | B2B-focused niches |
| Exchange structure | Direct or 3-way (ABC) | Direct, one-for-one |

## Where RankChase still wins

RankChase covers far more categories than just B2B (lifestyle, tech, local
services, and more), and the ABC exchange option can be useful if a direct
1-for-1 link with your best-matched partner doesn't make sense contextually.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

RankChase gates the useful part — actually seeing who you matched with —
behind a per-site monthly fee. Exchange Backlinks shows you the match and
lets you talk directly with them, free, from the first email. If your site
is B2B, the niche pool is also more targeted.
