## What is RankChase?

[RankChase](https://www.rankchase.com/) automatically matches you with websites in your niche and Domain Rating range that are open to backlink exchanges, including 3-way (ABC) exchanges. It supports 30+ categories and lets you select up to 5. The free tier only notifies you that a match exists; a Premium plan ($19/month per website) unlocks full transparency on who you're matched with, ABC exchanges, and filtering by DR and traffic. An Agency plan with custom pricing covers bulk domain import for multiple sites.

## RankChase vs Exchange Backlinks

| | RankChase | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | Backlink exchange only | Backlink matching only |
| Price | Free tier + $19/mo/website Premium | Free |
| Seeing who you're matched with | Paid feature | Included from the start |
| Niche coverage | 30+ categories, general | B2B-focused niches |
| Exchange structure | Direct or 3-way (ABC) | Direct, one-for-one |

## Where RankChase still wins

RankChase covers far more categories than just B2B — lifestyle, local services, tech, and more — and the ABC exchange option can be useful if a direct 1-for-1 link with your best-matched partner doesn't make sense contextually. It's a better fit if your site sits outside B2B entirely, or you're managing exchanges across several domains via the Agency plan.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

RankChase gates the useful part — actually seeing who you matched with — behind a per-site monthly fee. [Submit your site](/submit) to Exchange Backlinks instead and you see the match and talk directly with them, free, from the first email. If your site is B2B, the niche pool is also more targeted than RankChase's general 30-category list. For other niche-matched exchanges, see [KarmaLinks](/alternatives/karmalinks) and [Authority Exchange](/alternatives/authority-exchange).

## Frequently asked questions

### Is RankChase free?

The base tier is free but only sends match notifications — you don't see who you've been matched with unless you upgrade to Premium at $19/month per website.

### How much does RankChase Premium cost?

$19/month per website for full transparency (names and URLs), ABC exchanges, and DR/traffic filtering. Multi-domain teams can get custom Agency pricing instead.

### What's the best free RankChase alternative?

Exchange Backlinks shows you the full match — not just a notification — for free, and is specifically focused on B2B niches rather than RankChase's broader category list. Compare more options among the [free alternatives](/alternatives).

### Does RankChase support ABC (3-way) exchanges?

Yes, but only on the Premium plan. The free tier is limited to notifications about direct matches.
