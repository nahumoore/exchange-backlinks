## What is Distribb?

Distribb is an AI content platform — it generates and publishes SEO-optimized
articles on a schedule, and bundles a backlink exchange as part of the
subscription. Once enabled, your site starts receiving contextual links from
other Distribb customers' articles, and your published content links back to
theirs in return.

## Distribb vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Distribb | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | AI article generation + publishing | Backlink matching only |
| Backlink exchange | Bundled feature, requires the content plan | The entire product |
| Price | From $97/mo after a $9 first month | Free |
| Who picks the link partner | Automated placement across the network | You — both sides agree before URLs are shared |
| Niche matching | Broad, cross-industry network | B2B niches only |

## Where Distribb still wins

If you already want an AI writer churning out 30 articles a month, getting a
backlink exchange bundled in is convenient — you're not managing two tools.
Distribb is a fit for teams whose bottleneck is content volume, not link
building specifically.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

If you don't need (or don't trust) AI-generated content, paying $97+/month
for a content subscription just to get a backlink exchange is a lot of
overhead for one feature. Exchange Backlinks does the one thing — matching
you with a real B2B site in your niche, once a week, so you can trade a link
you both actually want on it.
