## What is Distribb?

[Distribb](https://distribb.io/) is an AI content platform that generates and publishes SEO-optimized articles on a schedule, and bundles a backlink exchange into the subscription. Once enabled, your site starts receiving contextual links from other Distribb customers' published articles, and your own content links back to theirs in return — no manual outreach on either side. Plans start at $97/month (with a promotional $9 first month), and Distribb estimates Domain Rating movement from the exchange within 2 to 4 weeks of turning it on.

## Where Distribb still wins

If you already want an AI writer churning out 30 articles a month, getting a backlink exchange bundled in is convenient — you're not managing two tools. Distribb is the better fit for teams whose actual bottleneck is content volume and who are comfortable letting an automated network decide where their links land, not link building specifically.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

If you don't need (or don't fully trust) AI-generated content, paying $97+/month for a content subscription just to get a backlink exchange is a lot of overhead for one feature. [Submit your site](/submit) to Exchange Backlinks instead, and you get the one thing that matters: a real B2B site in your niche, matched once a week, with a link you both actually agreed to place. Teams comparing AI-bundled exchanges also tend to look at [Outrank](/alternatives/outrank) and [Arvow](/alternatives/arvow), which run a similar model at different price points.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Distribb free?

No. Distribb's cheapest plan is $97/month, discounted to $9 for the first month only. The backlink exchange only runs while you're subscribed to the content plan — there's no way to access just the exchange on its own.

### What's the best free Distribb alternative?

Exchange Backlinks is free with no subscription. You're matched weekly with a specific B2B site in your niche and negotiate the placement directly, rather than an algorithm placing links across a broader, cross-industry network. See the full list of [free alternatives](/alternatives) if you're comparing more than one option.

### Does Distribb's backlink exchange work without the AI content plan?

No — the exchange is a feature bundled into the content subscription, not a standalone product you can buy on its own.

### How fast do Distribb's backlink exchange results show up?

Distribb estimates Domain Rating movement within 2–4 weeks of enabling the exchange, though actual timing depends on how many other customers are active in your content niche at the time.
