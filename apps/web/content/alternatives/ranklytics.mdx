## What is Ranklytics?

[Ranklytics](https://ranklytics.ai/features/backlink-exchange/) is an AI SEO content tool whose Backlink Exchange feature matches your site with topically relevant peers in its network and coordinates the placement — both sides get an editorial backlink automatically once a match is confirmed. Ranklytics says it screens for genuine topical relevance rather than running a link farm or mass cross-linking scheme. Plans run roughly $19–79/month depending on content volume, with lifetime deals occasionally seen around $59.

## Where Ranklytics still wins

If content writing is your actual bottleneck, having a backlink exchange come bundled with the writing tool means one less subscription to manage — a reasonable trade for teams that want links to happen passively alongside their content pipeline.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Ranklytics free?

No. Ranklytics plans start around $19/month and scale with content volume; the backlink exchange is a feature of the subscription, not a free standalone tool.

### What's the best free Ranklytics alternative?

Exchange Backlinks: no subscription, no content plan required. You get one weekly email matched to your B2B niche and agree on the placement directly with your match. See the rest of the [free alternatives](/alternatives) for more comparisons.

### Do I need Ranklytics' content plan to use its backlink exchange?

Yes — the exchange is bundled into the paid subscription and isn't sold as a standalone feature.

### How does Ranklytics decide who I get matched with?

It uses topical relevance signals from your published content to find peers in its network, then coordinates the placement automatically — you don't see or approve individual matches before the link goes live.
