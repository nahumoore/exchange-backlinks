## What is Ranklytics?

Ranklytics is an AI SEO content tool whose backlink exchange feature matches
your site with topically relevant peers in its network and coordinates the
placement — both sides get an editorial backlink automatically once a match
is confirmed.

## Ranklytics vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Ranklytics | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core product | AI content writing | Backlink matching only |
| Backlink exchange | Bundled feature | The entire product |
| Price | Plans roughly $19–79/mo, lifetime deals seen at $59 | Free |
| Who arranges the placement | Automated by Ranklytics | You and your matched partner, directly |
| Niche matching | General topical relevance | B2B niches only |

## Where Ranklytics still wins

If content writing is your actual bottleneck, having a backlink exchange
come along with the writing tool means one less subscription to manage.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

You don't need an AI writing subscription just to trade links. Exchange
Backlinks is free standalone, and because you talk to your match directly,
you control exactly where and how the link goes live — not an automated
placement you only see after the fact.
