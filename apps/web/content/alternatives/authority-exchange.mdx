## What is Authority Exchange?

Authority Exchange is a link exchange marketplace connecting brands,
publishers, and link builders through a credit system — no direct payment
per link. Each link is assigned a credit value based on the site's Ahrefs
DR, and links must stay live for at least six months. Building links itself
is free, but the platform runs on a monthly subscription fee.

## Authority Exchange vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Authority Exchange | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core model | Credit-based exchange marketplace | Free reciprocal exchange |
| Price | Monthly subscription fee (links themselves are free) | Free, no subscription |
| Vetting | Highly vetted brands and publishers | Any B2B site in a supported niche |
| Link duration requirement | Minimum 6 months, enforced | Agreed directly between members |
| Niche matching | General, cross-industry | B2B niches only |

## Where Authority Exchange still wins

The vetting bar and enforced 6-month minimum mean links on Authority
Exchange tend to be more consistently high-quality across a broad set of
industries — useful if you're not exclusively B2B.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

Authority Exchange still charges a subscription to access the credit
marketplace. Exchange Backlinks has no subscription at all — you submit
your site, get matched weekly with a real site in your niche, and agree on
the swap yourselves.
