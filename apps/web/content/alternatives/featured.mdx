## What is Featured.com?

[Featured.com](https://www.featured.com/) (formerly Terkel) is a two-sided marketplace: publishers post questions, and experts answer them for a chance to be quoted — and linked — in the resulting article. It offers a free tier plus paid plans (Premium around $99/month) that add an AI co-pilot to surface and auto-respond to opportunities across platforms. In April 2025, Featured.com acquired the HARO brand from Cision and relaunched it as a free email digest after Cision's paid "Connectively" rebrand was rejected by users — so Featured now effectively runs both formats.

There's a free tier, but it's limited compared to Premium (~$99/month), which adds AI-assisted response tools and broader visibility into opportunities.

### Does Featured.com now own HARO?
