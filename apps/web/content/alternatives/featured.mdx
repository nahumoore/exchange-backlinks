## What is Featured.com?

Featured.com (formerly Terkel) is a two-sided marketplace: publishers post
questions, and experts answer them for a chance to be quoted — and linked —
in the resulting article. It offers a free tier plus paid plans (Premium
around $99/mo) that add an AI co-pilot to surface and auto-respond to
opportunities across platforms.

## Featured.com vs Exchange Backlinks

| | Featured.com | Exchange Backlinks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| What's exchanged | Expert answers for possible article inclusion | Backlinks, directly |
| Backlink outcome | Depends on being selected and credited | Guaranteed by the match |
| Price | Free tier is limited; Premium ~$99/mo | Free, no tiers |
| Effort per link | Answer questions, wait to be picked | Agree with a matched site, place the link |
| Niche | Cross-industry topics | B2B niches only |

## Where Featured.com still wins

Featured's free tier is genuinely limited — real reach needs the paid tier
— but even then, you're building a public expert profile across many
publications, not just accumulating links. That's worth something on its
own if visibility, not just SEO, is the goal.

## Why teams switch to Exchange Backlinks

Featured.com's meaningful results sit behind a ~$99/month plan, and even
then a link isn't guaranteed — it depends on being selected. Exchange
Backlinks costs nothing and the match itself is the deal: no pitching into
the void hoping to get picked.
